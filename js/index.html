About me


¡Hello Everybody!


My name is Helen Bonilla and I'm a Developer and UI/UX Designer from Colombia, currently based in Cali, Colombia.

I am a lifelong learner, always excited to work on cutting-edge technologies, ready to help, and passionate about the things I do every day.


I have always loved designing and creating things with code since I was a Software Engineering student at the Universidad del Valle where I discovered my passion for web development and UX design during the last years of my degree, in fact, I started my first job there.

I have been working in web development since 2019, I have faced various types of projects, such as e-commerce, social networks and Multitenant platforms (SaaS).


I also really enjoy being part of programming communities where I have organized events such as local meetups with PionerasDev and Django Girls Workshops. Sometimes I give some talks.

Welcome to my page and do not hesitate to consult each section in which you want to delve deeper about me, if you have any questions do not hesitate to contact me.
